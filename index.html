Privacy Policy for Pomood

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Pomood ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Pomood mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Pomood does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

The app operates entirely on your device. All data, including:

  • Timer state
  • Pomodoro count
  • Timer settings

is stored locally on your device using local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not backed up to cloud services
  • Is not transmitted over the internet
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

Pomood does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
